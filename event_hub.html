Ideathon

An ideathon is a structured, collaborative event where individuals or teams gather to brainstorm and develop innovative solutions to specific challenges. Unlike hackathons that focus on coding or building physical prototypes, ideathons are conceptual. They emphasize research, problem framing, and pitching ideas to stakeholders.
